Dolphin and Whale Watching

The waters of the archipelago are home to an extremely rich fauna. Dolphin and Whale Watching is one of the most exciting activities you can experience when visiting Madeira.

Here are some of the species you can spot:

  • Spotted dolphin (Stenella frontalis);

  • Bottlenose dolphin (Tursiops truincatu);

  • Pilot Whale (Globicephala macrorhynchus);

  • Bryde's whale (Balaenoptera edeni);

  • Loggerhead turtle (Caretta caretta).

Source: VisitMadeira
