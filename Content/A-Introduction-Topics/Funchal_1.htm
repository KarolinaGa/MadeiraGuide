Funchal

It's Madeira's largest tourist, commercial and cultural center. Funchal has a lot to offer. Secluded viewpoints with breathtaking views, botanical and tropical gardens, historical buildings and museums.

Here is a list of some places worth visiting :

  • Monte Palace Tropical Garden (Click to see)
  • Botanical Garden (You can get to both gardens by Funchal Cable Car!)
  • Funchal Cathedral
  • Santa Caterina Park
  • Funchal Cable Car and many others!
