Viewpoints

Madeira is a great destination for hikers. Go for a hike and enjoy breathtaking views. Here is a list of some of the best viewpoints:

  • Pico do Areeiro Viewpoint
  • Pico Ruivo Viewpoint
  • Balcões Viewpoint
  • Cabanas Viewpoint
  • Rocha do Navio Viewpoint
  • Ponta do Pargo Lighthouse Viewpoint and many others!

Source: Viewpoint list, VisitMadeira
